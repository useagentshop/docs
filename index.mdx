AgentShop makes your Shopify storefront legible to AI shopping agents and search engines — server-rendered structured data, meta tags, and sitemaps that crawlers can actually read.

These docs currently cover the **Headless SEO SDK** (`@agentshop/seo`), which brings your AgentShop-generated SEO into headless storefronts on Next.js, Remix, and Hydrogen.
